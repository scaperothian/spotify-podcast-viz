Welcome to Our Spotify Podcast Exploration Site

Dive deep into the world of podcasts on Spotify through our interactive visualizations and insights.

Our curated dataset offers a detailed look at over 100,000 episodes spanning diverse shows and emanating from an array of publishers. This provides a holistic perspective on Spotify's podcast domain.

For a clearer understanding, let's elucidate some key terminologies:

  • Publisher: These are the studios pivotal to the podcast world. They operate multiple podcast shows, akin to production houses in cinema, creating and broadcasting a range of podcast content.
  • Show: This refers to a podcast series. Akin to a TV series, a podcast show is a collection of episodes centered around a main theme or topic.
  • Episodes: These are individual segments of long-form audio that form part of a show. Think of them as chapters in a book, each adding to the overarching story.

Join us in this exploration and uncover the multifaceted realm of Spotify's podcasts. Whether you're a podcast aficionado, data enthusiast, or simply curious, there's a wealth of knowledge awaiting you!
